chicken bruschetta pasta

Prep: 20 min Total: 20 min

Makes 5 servings

you will need:

  • 2 Tbsp. KRAFT Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 pkg. (16.4 oz.) KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Chicken Bruschetta Pasta
  • 1-1/2 lb. boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 3 cups farfalle (bow-tie pasta), uncooked

directions:

  1. COOK pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
  2. MEANWHILE, cook and stir chicken in half the Simmer Sauce in large skillet until done.
  3. DRAIN pasta. Add to skillet with remaining Simmer Sauce; mix lightly.
  4. TOP with Finishing Sauce and cheese; cover. Cook on low heat 2 to 3 min. or until heated through.

kitchen tips:

  • SERVING SUGGESTION Serve with a quick-bagged salad tossed with your favorite KRAFT Dressing, such as House Italian.
  • SUBSTITUTE Substitute 1 lb. cumbled Italian sausage or lean ground beef for the chicken. Brown meat in skillet; drain. Add all the Simmer Sauce and continue as directed.
  • SPECIAL EXTRA Top with fresh basil and croutons just before serving.
